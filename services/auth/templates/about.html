Our System

Applications in the field of health have evolved with the needs of the user and their capabilities have increased. Accurate disease diagnosis with methods based on artificial intelligence is one of the current issues of this system. Apart from the diagnosis of the disease, in many cases the patient does not know which doctor he should see and which specialist. This confusion may lead to referrals to irrelevant specialists and a lot of wasted time. In this project, the healthcare system for disease diagnosis and referral to a specialist has been designed and prototyped using microservice architecture. The system is analyzed into small and independent services that can be developed and maintained in the future. In this project, you can meet your requirements using the user interface or the service interface using the connection program and connecting to the services. The implemented services are: user login and profile service, disease diagnosis and expertise service, expert search service.

System Design

Software architecture has always been an important part of design and development. Microservices architecture, as an indicative style of architectural software that was created with the philosophy of separating system components into smaller services, provides many solutions for large design and implementation.
